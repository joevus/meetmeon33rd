Our Story

Meet Me on 33rd was born in 2012, when this 1932 home was updated and remodeled for your use. The charm convenience and various big rooms and kitchen make it feel like home. Schedule a tour and imagine the ease of getting a group together without the hassles of getting your home ready: the cleaning, what to do with the pets, disrupting family schedules and parking issues and just not having quite enough space. We can help. Schedule your tour now.

Our Community

Every week yoga and meditation groups, support groups, baby showers, business meetings or trainings, book clubs and knitting circles, networking gatherings and parties are hosted by many different businesses, non-profits, neighbors and friends. Join us and feel right at home.

Check your schedule and see how Meet Me on 33rd can help you host a gathering in the future.

In addition to event and meeting space there are eight offices occupied by wonderful and amazing people who call Meet Me home.

Meat the Team

Meet Me on 33rd was created by Stephanie just for you to make your event unique and memorable. Recommendations for catering, bartending, flowers and supplies are available. Once you have reserved the space, you are on your own for setting up and cleaning up, just like home. You get to select your own team members.
