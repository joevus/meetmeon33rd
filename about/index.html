Our story

Meet Me On 33rd came into life in 2012. People booked it for parties, business meeting. Soon word began to spread. They appreciated the cleanliness, the charm, and conveniences, not to mention the spacious rooms and awesome kitchen.

Our Community

From yoga to business conferences people come to enjoy the space at Meet Me on 33rd. We're proud of the sense of community that has years to draw from, yet seems to hit you the day you step into the space.

Several businesses, part of this community have set up residence in the space.
